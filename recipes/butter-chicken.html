Butter Chicken Recipe

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) is a rich North Indian curry of tandoori-style chicken simmered in a creamy, mildly spiced tomato gravy. Beloved for its smooth, buttery texture, it pairs perfectly with naan or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Sear the marinated chicken in a hot pan until lightly charred, then set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt the butter and cook the tomato puree with spices until the oil separates.
  4. Stir in the cream, add the chicken, and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with a swirl of cream and serve hot with naan or rice.
